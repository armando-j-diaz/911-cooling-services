1. Program Description

J.E.T Mobile Engineering Services / 911 Cooling / Jet AC operates an SMS messaging program to communicate with customers regarding scheduled HVAC service appointments. Messages are transactional and operational in nature — no promotional or marketing content is sent through this program.

2. Operator

This messaging program is operated by J.E.T Mobile Engineering Services / 911 Cooling / Jet AC. For support, contact us at javier@911coolingservices.com.

3. Consent

By accepting HVAC service from J.E.T Mobile Engineering Services / 911 Cooling / Jet AC as a contractor assigned through American Home Shield, you consent to receive SMS messages necessary for the completion of your service appointment. Consent is not a condition of any purchase.

4. Message Types

You may receive the following types of messages:

  • Technician dispatch and arrival notifications
  • Links to service intake forms
  • Appointment reminders
  • Post-service follow-ups

5. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You may receive up to 5 messages per service request.

6. Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Contact your carrier for details.

7. Opt-Out

Reply STOP at any time to cancel. You will receive one confirmation message and no further messages. Reply START to re-enroll.

8. Help

Reply HELP for help, or contact us at javier@911coolingservices.com.

9. Liability

J.E.T Mobile Engineering Services / 911 Cooling / Jet AC is not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Delivery is subject to your carrier's network availability.

10. Privacy

Your mobile number is never sold or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. See our full SMS Privacy Policy for details.
