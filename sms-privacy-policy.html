SMS Privacy Policy

J.E.T Mobile Engineering Services / 911 Cooling / Jet AC | Last updated: June 2, 2026

1. Who We Are

J.E.T Mobile Engineering Services / 911 Cooling / Jet AC is an HVAC contractor serving customers dispatched through American Home Shield and direct service requests. This policy covers how we collect and use mobile phone numbers in connection with our SMS messaging program.

2. How We Collect Your Phone Number

We receive your contact information — including your mobile phone number — when you file a service request with American Home Shield and J.E.T Mobile Engineering Services / 911 Cooling / Jet AC is assigned as your contractor. By accepting service from a contractor under your American Home Shield warranty, you consent to receive SMS messages necessary to complete your service appointment.

3. How We Use Your Phone Number

We use your mobile number solely to send transactional and operational messages related to your service appointment. These messages may include:

  • Technician dispatch and arrival notifications
  • Links to intake forms to collect job-site information
  • Appointment reminders
  • Post-service follow-ups

4. No Sharing of Mobile Numbers

We do not sell, rent, share, or transfer your mobile phone number to any third party for marketing or promotional purposes. Your number is used exclusively for service-related communication as described above.

5. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your service appointment. You may receive up to 5 messages per service request.

6. Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Contact your mobile carrier for details on your plan's messaging rates.

7. How to Opt Out

You may opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us. After opting out, you will receive one confirmation message and no further messages. To re-enroll, reply START.

8. Help

For help, reply HELP to any message or contact us at javier@911coolingservices.com.
