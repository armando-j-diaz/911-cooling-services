SMS Terms & Conditions

Jet AC (J.E.T Mobile Engineering Services LLC) | Last updated: June 3, 2026

1. Program Description

Jet AC operates an SMS messaging program to communicate with customers regarding scheduled HVAC service appointments. Messages are transactional and operational in nature — no promotional or marketing content is sent through this program.

2. Operator

This messaging program is operated by Jet AC, a DBA of J.E.T Mobile Engineering Services LLC. For support, contact us at javier@911coolingservices.com.

3. Consent

When Jet AC is assigned to your American Home Shield service request, you will receive a single SMS notification asking you to reply YES to opt in to appointment updates. By replying YES, you consent to receive SMS messages necessary for the completion of your service appointment. Consent is not a condition of any purchase or service.

4. Message Types

After opting in, you may receive the following types of messages:

  • Technician dispatch and arrival notifications
  • Links to service intake forms
  • Appointment reminders
  • Post-service follow-ups

5. Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. You may receive up to 5 messages per service request.

6. Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Contact your carrier for details.

7. Opt-Out

Reply STOP at any time to cancel. You will receive one confirmation message and no further messages. Reply START to re-enroll.

8. Help

Reply HELP for help, or contact us at javier@911coolingservices.com.

9. Liability

Jet AC is not liable for delayed or undelivered messages. Delivery is subject to your carrier's network availability.

10. Privacy

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. See our full SMS Privacy Policy for details.
